IMPORTANT!

CloudCharge will use your default Braintree merchant account for transactions if you do not provide a merchant account id. Therefore, CloudCharge will transact ONLY in the currency of your default Braintree merchant account, REGARDLESS of the CloudCharge currency setting. Please be sure that your currency is set to the currency of your default merchant account if not using a merchant account id, or currency of the merchant account id provided. Otherwise your customers will be shown the incorrect currency within CloudCharge.

Note:
Braintree has two different APIs. If you were provided with a Login and Password rather than a Merchant ID, public key and private key, you will need to contact Support to enable the legacy v1 API.
